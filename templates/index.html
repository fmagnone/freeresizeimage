How we do it?

Free Resize Image is an online app that allows you to compress your image online to adjust quality and optimize information. You could drag and drop or simple upload any image you have by clicking on the uploader. When you upload your image, our app will show you old and new format, size, and resolution. Typical formats like BMP, GIF, JPG or PNG are supported. You could also upload other image formats like iPhone HEIC files. The uploader is fast and secure and all the process is made online without storing information in any server.
